§01 Premise

The bracket your supplier doesn’t make. The fixture your machinist won’t quote.

A short note

Most manufacturing problems aren’t solved by a catalog or a machine shop. They’re solved by a single-quantity bracket, a one-off jig, or an obsolete replacement part — work too small for a CNC quote and too specific for an off-the-shelf solution.

Everhaven exists to close that gap. We design and produce custom parts in carbon-fiber-reinforced composite on industrial-grade additive systems, with the dimensional discipline of an engineering shop and the lead times of a service bureau. Send a drawing, a sketch, or a photograph with dimensions. We’ll send back a part you can put to work.
